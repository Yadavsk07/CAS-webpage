SCHOOL OF COMPUTER SCIENCE AND ENGINEERING

The School of Computer Science & Engineering is a unique school that leverages the high-quality programs of Xavier University and emphasizes sound computer science fundamentals, coding, research, innovation, and entrepreneurship. Established in 2017, the School of Computer Science & Engineering focuses on innovations in Computer Science as they play an important role in most emerging technologies. It offers a B.Tech. in Computer Science & Engineering, an M.Tech. in Data Science & Analytics, and a Ph.D. in Computer Science & Engineering. Its current faculty mostly has Ph.D. and/or postdoctoral experience from IITs/IISc or abroad. The founding Dean, Prof. Priyadarsan Patra, has a Ph.D. from the University of Texas at Austin and 20+ years of leadership experience at Intel (USA). It is being mentored by Prof. Chitta Baral from Arizona State University, Prof. Prasant Mohapatra from the University of California Davis, and Dr. Ashutosh Dutta of Johns Hopkins University.The Department has over 250 students and around 20 faculty members, including core and guest faculty members. Its youthful and dynamic faculty, passionate students, and dedicated staff envision building a premier institute with the foundational intent of innovating and cultivating ideas.

B.TECH IN CSE(HONS.)

The B.Tech. in Computer Science and Engineering is a four-year, full-time program designed to provide ample opportunity for students to learn coding languages, develop and analyze algorithms, understand systems, and implement software in various programming languages and platforms. Teaching methodology consists of lecture inputs, exercises, assignments, design capstone, thesis/project work, presentations, etc. Each instructor uses a suitable pedagogy to suit the requirements of the course. Students in a course are likewise evaluated in various components, viz. assignment, quiz, laboratory exercise, midsemester examination, end-semester examination, and other assignments. Course outline/session plan for every course specifying the contents of the course, the methodology, and the norms for evaluation are uploaded in the UMS at the beginning of a semester. The CSE program consists of eight semesters spread over four academic years. Each academic year consists of two semesters and the duration of each semester is approximately five months.

PROJECTS , CLUBS AND ACTIVITIES

PROJECTS

Students are required to do group projects/research in many of the courses that they are taught. Students are also provided with an option of independent study which enables them to do research in the field of their interest such as Data Mining, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Big Data, Soft Computing, Network/Cloud Configuration and Deployment, Web and App Development.

CLUBS

Community participation in college sets the stage for a lifetime of learning in, reaching and building connections. The School of Computer Science & Engineering has its own clubs; XPLORE (Coding Club), Media Cell, Robogenix, the IEEE Student Chapter and the ACM Student Chapter.

CLUBS

XPLORE

XPLORE is with the objective of helping all students indulge in the world of computer science with full gusto and gain useful information and knowledge about computer science. It also organizes coding competitions and hackathons. School of Computer Science & Engineering was also successful in conducting the CALL FOR CODE hackathon by IBM. Students of School of Computer Science & Engineering organized the first XAl summit at XIM University, Bhubaneswar and then a code camp for high school students.

MEDIA CELL

This club is set up with a vision of building a strong framework of social networking with the world. It comes up with many engrossing tasks and events to keep an active social life of School of Computer Science & Engineering. This club will give a virtual platform to enfold School of Computer Science & Engineering talents in front of social media viewers. Apart from that this club is mainly initiated to preserve the valuable captured memories of our school events as well to showcase the inside talents and achievements within the mass audience.

ACM CHAPTER

ACM-XIM Chapter is a leading technical student chapter in XIM University, committed to inspiring young tech enthusiasts to explore the imitless potential of technology. With the support of the Association for Computing Machinery (ACM), the chapter has organized a range of exciting events in web development, loT, AI, ML, Cloud Computing, and more. The chapter has quickly become a hub for innovation and learning in technology, attracting some of the brightest and most talented students from across the university.

ROBOGENIX

RobogeniX is the first club based on Robotics and I0T which provides students with ample opportunities to show their potential and interests through events and competitions, interaction with seniors and mentors, workshops and hands on training on loT Devices and lab equipment access. Exciting events like quiz competition on Robotics and obstacle course race for bots are organized regularly.

IEEE CHAPTER

The IEEE-XIM Student Chapter at XIM University is a premier technical student organization dedicated to fostering innovation and excellence in engineering and technology. Supported by the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (|EEE), the chapter organizes a diverse array of events and activities, including workshops, seminars, and hackathons in areas such as electronics, telecommunications, software engineering, and power systems. By providing opportunities for hands-on learning, professional development, and networking, the IEEE-XIM Student Chapter empowers students to push the boundaries of technology and contribute to advancements in their fields.

HOW TO REACH

This campus is at Kurki-Nijigada under Pipli Tahsil, situated at a distance of 20 km from our old campus (15.1 km from the Biju Patnaik International Airport) and 17.0 km from Bhubaneswar Railway Station. One can book a taxi from the airport or railway station to reach the campus or can use public transport (MoBus No. 22A that starts from Master Canteen) to reach XIM University, Bhubaneswar.

Biju Patnaik International Airport (Bhubaneswar Airport) has direct flight for all the major cities in India. It is 15.1 Km away from the Univ. Campus.

Bhubaneswar railway station is 17.0 Km from Univ. campus. It has trains for all the major destinations in India.

We would be glad to make arrangements for your stay at several three to five star hotels in Bhubaneswar.
